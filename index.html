Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries about our IT services.

Do you offer remote IT support outside of India?
Absolutely. While headquartered in Delhi, we are a cloud-first company. We provide 24/7 global remote support to clients across North America, Europe, and Asia Pacific using secure, encrypted remote management tools.
What does the licensing consultation process look like?
Our experts conduct a deep-dive audit of your current Microsoft 365/Azure tenant. We map your actual usage against your current SKUs, identify overlapping software (like paying for Zoom when you have Teams), and recommend license downgrades or consolidations that typically save our clients 15-30% annually.
Is Microsoft 365 secure out of the box?
No. The default settings in M365 prioritize ease-of-use over security. We implement critical baselines, including enforced Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA), Conditional Access policies (blocking logins from unauthorized countries), and advanced phishing protection via Defender to truly secure your tenant.
What is included in your IT Training for freshers?
Our bootcamps are intensely practical. Led by Amit Reddy, we cover real-world scenarios: setting up Exchange routing, configuring Intune policies, troubleshooting AVD, and preparing for essential Microsoft certifications (like MS-900 or SC-300) to ensure immediate job readiness.
